If we take this one step further, we end up with what we now a days refer to as functions. Instead of contextually grouping behavior together into a service, we split it out and let each behavior have it's own function. Basically, every http endpoint, job or what have you becomes their own microservice. You want to create a purchase order? Well, there's a service for that! You want to print your purchase order? There's another service for that!
