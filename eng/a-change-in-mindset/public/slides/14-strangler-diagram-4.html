This is of course no magic silver bullet, and your mileage may vary. But out of the different patterns and reference architectures I've examined, evaluated or tried this by far qualify as the most powerful one. The risk is low, you maintain all your current functionality while still being able to deliver rapidly and move chunks out of the monolith as needed. If there is one single thing that I'd like you to take with you from this talk, this is probably it.
