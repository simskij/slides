So how does this work? Well, you, as the tester - or the scientist if you will - design an experiment based on a hypothesis. Utilising drivers, you then connect to your resources and inject different kinds of failure, probing the system to make sure the degradation stays within the bounds you've defined. If you're able to perform all the actions you've defined as part of your experiment while still staying within your boundary, or error budget, the text is considered successful. If not, it's considered a fail. The results, regardless of the outcome, can then, using plugins, be sent to a service of your choice, for storage or producing notifications.
